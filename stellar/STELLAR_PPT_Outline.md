## Design Notes for PPT Creation:

**Color Scheme:**
- Primary: Deep space blue (#0a0a1a)
- Accent: Electric cyan (#00d4ff)
- Secondary: Warm gold (#ffa500)
- Text: White on dark backgrounds

**Fonts:**
- Headings: Inter or Outfit (Bold)
- Body: Inter or Roboto (Regular)

**Imagery:**
- Use satellite/space imagery as backgrounds
- Include actual UI screenshots from the platform
- Use icons for features lists

**Animations:**
- Subtle fade-ins for text
- Slide transitions: Morph or Push
- Keep professional, not flashy
